Expanding billboard

Pencil leave behind advertisement

Oct. 21, 2009

Features:

  • Communicates with javascript/jQuery.
  • “clickTAG” support (RG uses OAS OpenX).
  • HTTP cookie support.
  • Easily specify the tease and main advertisement (defaults are “tease.swf” & “advert.swf”).
  • Control the timing of the the “open” state.
  • Flexible width/height.
  • Fluid animation(s).

Docs:

  • Photoshop guide document.
  • “tease” Flash template.
  • “advert” Flash template.
